Hungry Ghost by Susannah Jones (msj6nb), Amanda Seiken (ams5xy), Will Savino (wps3ua)

*****CONTROLS******

G - Activate Ghost Powers
Arrow Keys - Move
Q - Quit

Move throughout the house to grab all the fruit! You need to be visible to eat the fruit, but 
make sure the owner of the house isn't in the same room as you when you turn visible!


Requirements for Alpha:
-Working base/starter level
-Home owner & AI/Paths
-Health bar based on fruit acquired


For the beta, we will have the final level working & completed, as well as art/music 
updated, an intermediate level, and a losing feature.
